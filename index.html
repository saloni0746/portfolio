About Saloni

A Health Assistant Driven
by Care & Commitment

"Every individual deserves clear health guidance, compassionate support, and the tools to take charge of their own wellbeing."

Saloni Yadav is a certified health assistant and wellness advisor with over 3 years of experience supporting patients and communities across healthcare settings in West Bengal. Her work spans patient care coordination, health education, chronic disease support, and preventive wellness.

With a warm, approachable style and a commitment to health literacy, Saloni bridges the gap between clinical care and everyday healthy living. She is known for her patient advocacy, her clear communication of medical guidance, and her passion for empowering people with the knowledge to make informed health choices.
